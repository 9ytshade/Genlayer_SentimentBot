# GenLayer Launches Incentivized Testnet Asimov: The Court of the Internet Activates

_June 17, 2025_ — San Francisco, CA — GenLayer, the AI-driven Court of the Internet, today launches Testnet Asimov, the first stage of its incentivized validator program and a key milestone in the journey toward the mainnet. Asimov activates the world’s first AI-powered blockchain court, marking the operational debut of Intelligent Contracts and the Optimistic Democracy consensus model.

Built for the machine age, GenLayer is a decentralized synthetic jurisdiction where autonomous agents and humans can resolve disputes, enforce contracts, and govern trustlessly, transparently, and at machine speed. 

"We're activating the network and starting the process of stress testing multiple variables to ensure we can support all the development we want on it," said Albert Castellana, GenLayer CEO and Co-Founder. "This is a huge signal to our community, validators, and developers, that the network is progressing toward mainnet and we're building something truly extraordinary.".

## **Multi-Stage Testnet Program**

Testnet Asimov is the first of two pre-mainnet Testnets designed to validate GenLayer’s novel "Optimistic Democracy" consensus mechanism. Unlike traditional blockchain consensus mechanisms that rely purely on deterministic code execution, GenLayer's validators are connected to different Large Language Models (LLMs) that work together to make subjective decisions and interpret real-world data. 

Testnet Asimov features a transparent and incentivized point system that rewards validators for active participation across network operations, testing activities, ecosystem and community contributions, and educational content. The validator program is for experienced professional validators with demonstrated expertise in blockchain infrastructure management, progressively expanding validator autonomy as the network approaches mainnet readiness.

The GenLayer testnet campaign is structured as a two-stage rollout:

- **Asimov**: Tests core network mechanics and validator onboarding. GenLayer provides subsidized LLM access to stress-test transaction execution, validator behavior, and consensus pathways.  
    
- **Bradbury**: Validators select and fine-tune their preferred LLMs, optimizing for inference quality and performance. Focus shifts to validator-specific AI operations and real-world data interpretation.  
    

Each phase builds toward the full realization of GenLayer’s mission: programmable, AI-driven trust at scale.

## **Targeting Professional Validators**

GenLayer is seeking experienced, professional validators for the initial testnet phases. Interested validators must apply and selected validators will demonstrate: 

- Experience validating multiple blockchain networks
- Professional-grade infrastructure and operations
- LLM experience
- Commitment to full-time participation during testing phases

"We're focusing initially on professional validators given how hands-on  the process will be," noted Ivan Raskovsky, GenLayer Foundation’s Head of Ecosystem. "As we scale to our full thousand-validator network, we hope to expand to include solo validators and different types of participants."

Interested validators can apply at [genlayer.com/testnet](http://genlayer.com/testnet).

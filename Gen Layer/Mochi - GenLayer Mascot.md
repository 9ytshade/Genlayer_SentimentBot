## About GenLayer

[](https://github.com/genlayer-foundation/genlayer-mascot/blob/main/README.md#about-genlayer)

[GenLayer](https://genlayer.com/) is an AI-native blockchain protocol that functions as a synthetic jurisdiction for trustless decision-making. It enables decentralized dispute resolution and contract enforcement powered by validator nodes running diverse AI models.

**Key Features:**

- Intelligent Contracts written in Python
- AI Consensus for subjective decisions
- Real-world data integration without oracles
- Natural language processing capabilities

Learn more:

- Website: [genlayer.com](https://genlayer.com/)
- Documentation: [docs.genlayer.com](https://docs.genlayer.com/)

## About Mochi

[](https://github.com/genlayer-foundation/genlayer-mascot/blob/main/README.md#about-mochi)

Mochi is GenLayer's official mascot - a friendly AI-powered cat character that represents the innovative and approachable nature of the GenLayer protocol. With cyber-enhanced features and an adorable design, Mochi embodies the fusion of cutting-edge AI technology and user-friendly blockchain solutions.

## The Mochi Quest - Origin Story

[](https://github.com/genlayer-foundation/genlayer-mascot/blob/main/README.md#the-mochi-quest---origin-story)

Mochi was born from **The Mochi Quest**, a community design contest that invited GenLayer community members to create the official face of the protocol.

### Contest Details

[](https://github.com/genlayer-foundation/genlayer-mascot/blob/main/README.md#contest-details)

- **Duration**: October 29 - November 16, 2025
- **Prize Pool**: $500 USDC + 10,000 Discord XP
- **Requirements**: Front, back, and side views with optional pose illustrations
- **Submissions**: Community members submitted their creative interpretations

### Contest Winner

[](https://github.com/genlayer-foundation/genlayer-mascot/blob/main/README.md#contest-winner)

**kellyboom888** was selected as the winner for their outstanding design that perfectly captured GenLayer's vision of combining cutting-edge AI technology with an approachable, friendly character.

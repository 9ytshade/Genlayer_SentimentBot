# The Intelligent Oracle: Real-Time Data Access for the Next Generation of dApps

We are introducing the Intelligent Oracle, a new class of AI-powered Oracle capable of fetching any data from the internet and putting it on-chain to enable decentralized prediction markets, insurances, perpetuals without limits and many more use cases. Intelligent Oracle is the first product based on Intelligent Contracts, powered by the first Intelligent Blockchain: GenLayer.  Radix DLT, Etherisc, PredX, Delphi Bets and Provably are the first partners to use GenLayer’s Intelligent Oracle solution. 

## How does the Intelligent Oracle work?

The Intelligent Oracle is powered by **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, integrated directly into GenLayer’s **Optimistic Democracy** consensus mechanism. Validators connect to LLMs, enabling the network to process non-deterministic transactions by fetching data from the Internet. When a query is made, a lead validator generates a proposed result using an LLM, while other validators independently verify this output against the pre-set equivalence criteria.  Optimistic Democracy ensures all decisions are accurate, reliable, and secure. 

The Oracle operates seamlessly within the GenLayer blockchain, delivering decentralized, transparent data access while supporting cross-chain compatibility. The Intelligent Oracle will first be hosted on a permissioned local network while preparing for the GenLayer Testnet towards the end of the year. 

You can learn more about the Intelligent Oracle on this website: [intelligentoracle.com](http://intelligentoracle.com/).

## Why do we need the Intelligent Oracle? 

Current dApps are hungry for real-world data, but they can’t access it due to their limitation to on-chain information. Prediction markets, insurance claims, and financial derivatives require immediate, accurate, and sometimes subjective data from the Internet. Traditional Oracle solutions are inadequate—they rely on pre-defined datasets or manual intervention, which makes them slow, costly, and inflexible.

Platforms like UMA and Chainlink have attempted to address these limitations but fall short in key areas. For example, Polymarket, the largest decentralized prediction market, faces systemic issues: vague or poorly defined specifications lead to disputes, while UMA, used as the resolution oracle, imposes significant costs and delays. Resolving disputes can take up to **98 hours**, with costs reaching **$4,200 to $104,000** per market, depending on volume. Chainlink, constitutes a decentralized solution but struggles with real-time, arbitrary data fetching.**‍**

### **The Intelligent Oracle targets three high-growth sectors:**

**Prediction markets:** Valued at **$35 billion in 2022** and projected to reach **$67 billion by 2028**, prediction markets depend on accurate, timely data. Platforms like Polymarket, which hit **$500 million** in monthly volume in September, still struggle without reliable real-time data. GenLayer’s Intelligent Oracle automates market resolutions by scraping trusted web sources and defining specifications using LLMs, providing speed and accuracy unmatched by existing solutions.

**Insurance:** With **$5.6 trillion** in premium income, the insurance industry faces challenges in event-based claim processing. Hurricanes alone caused **$130 billion** in damages in 2022, underscoring the need for automation. The Intelligent Oracle enables fast, automated claim verification by fetching real-time data from the web, supporting parametric insurance and allowing payouts under pre-agreed conditions. Developers can tap into liquidity across various blockchains, streamlining decentralized insurance processes.

**Financial Derivatives:** Recorded a volume of $600 trillion and $13.2 trillion in the crypto derivatives market in 2024 and relies on up-to-the-second data. The Intelligent Oracle introduces real-time data gathering for innovative products like perpetual markets across diverse assets, including gold and stocks. Whether for options trading or contract management, Oracle ensures decisions are powered by accurate, trustworthy, and current information.
